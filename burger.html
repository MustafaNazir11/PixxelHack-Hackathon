Burger Menu

Cheese Burger

A timeless classic, the cheese burger was first introduced in the 1920s and has remained a fan favorite for its simplicity and flavor.

Ingredients: Beef, Cheese, Lettuce, Tomato, Bun

Nutrition: 500 kcal, 25g protein, 30g fat, 35g carbs

Double Patty

Inspired by American diners, the double patty burger doubles the meat for twice the satisfaction. A hearty favorite since the 1950s.

Ingredients: Double Beef, Cheese, Onion, Pickles, Bun

Nutrition: 750 kcal, 40g protein, 45g fat, 40g carbs

Veggie Delight

A modern vegetarian twist that became popular with the rise of plant-based diets. Loved by health-conscious burger lovers.

Ingredients: Veggie Patty, Lettuce, Tomato, Avocado, Bun

Nutrition: 400 kcal, 15g protein, 18g fat, 45g carbs

Spicy Zinger

Originating in the 90s, the spicy zinger added heat and crunch to the burger world. A hit among spice lovers worldwide.

Ingredients: Fried Chicken, Spicy Mayo, Lettuce, Bun

Nutrition: 600 kcal, 30g protein, 35g fat, 45g carbs
